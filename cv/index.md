## Curriculum Vitae / 个人简历

### 👤 Profile / 个人简介

I am an associate professor at the College of Physics, [Hebei Normal University](http://www.hebtu.edu.cn/), China. My research focuses on galaxy formation and evolution, combining galaxy surveys, integral field spectroscopy, numerical simulations, and mock observations to study how interactions, gas dynamics, star formation, chemical enrichment, and environment shape galaxies.

I received my Ph.D. in Astrophysics from the [Shanghai Astronomical Observatory, Chinese Academy of Sciences](http://www.shao.ac.cn/) in 2020, and joined Hebei Normal University in the same year.
