# Curriculum Vitae

## Profile

I am an associate professor at the College of Physics, [Hebei Normal University](http://www.hebtu.edu.cn/), China. My research focuses on the observational study of galaxy formation and evolution, especially galaxy interactions and mergers, gas kinematics, gas cycling, and integral field spectroscopy.

Before joining Hebei Normal University, I received my Ph.D. in Astrophysics from [Shanghai Astronomical Observatory, Chinese Academy of Sciences](http://www.shao.ac.cn/) in 2020.
